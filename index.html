About Instagram Cross-Posting

Automatic cross-posting to Instagram requires a Business or Creator account connected via the Instagram Graph API. This feature requires Meta developer app approval. For now, linking your account enables the cross-post toggle when creating posts — full automation can be enabled once API access is configured.
